About the Database

Interested in research? Find out more about Yale’s faculty through RDB!

The Research Database (RDB) is created by the Yale Undergraduate Research Association (YURA) as a cross-disciplinary database of Yale faculty with profile and research information. With department filters and search functions, the database is a collection of information about faculty members from all departments at Yale. It aims to provide Yale undergraduates of any disciplines a convenient, user-friendly, and more intuitive platform to find out about the work of faculty at Yale and look for potential undergraduate research mentors.

RDB is not meant to be a one-stop shop for learning about and connecting with faculty, but rather an integrated springboard for such. After looking for interesting faculty members, do go to their websites to learn about them and their research!

If you are looking for more resources and information about undergraduate research, fellowships and other related events, we've got you covered— head over to YURA's website!


How to Use

Log in to the listings through the "Log In" button above. On the listings page, you can find, for each faculty member, their:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Website
  • Affiliated departments, programs and/or schools
  • A brief description of their work and research (Use the "Show more/less" button to expand and contract the descriptions.)

To learn more about them, click on the name or "Go to website" to access the faculty members' webpage.

Also,

  • Use the keyword search box to search for names or keywords in descriptions
  • Use the “Departments” box to filter the list by their affiliated departments and programs
  • Click on "Reset Filter" to remove the searches and filters

Explore around the database to try out some of the features yourself!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this database for STEM students only?

No! RDB is made for Yalies of all disciplines, and faculty included in RDB spans across all of Yale's departments. We believe that students in all disciplines would find a research database resource useful.

Where do the listings come from? Are they accurate?

All listings items on RDB are taken from publicly available information about faculty in respective departments' websites, unless faculty members specifically requested to add, remove, or edit their entries.

Is this database available to the public?

No, only users with valid Yale NetID authentication are able to access this database.

Why are certain faculty members listed/unlisted?

All RDB entries are collected from faculty listings in undergraduate department websites, with a limited number of graduate school departments. Due to practical constraints, we are sorry if a department or faculty member was missed.

If you are faculty member and you would like to see yourself listed or removed, please fill out this simple request form, or email us directly at yura@yale.edu.

I'm a faculty member, and I want to edit my listing.

We are open to updates and changes on the listing entries. Send us a simple request form or simply send us an email at yura@yale.edu and we will process it at our earliest convenience.

I found bugs/I have suggestions for the database.

Let us know! Send us a message through the feedback form or send us an email at yura@yale.edu and we will respond as soon as we can.
